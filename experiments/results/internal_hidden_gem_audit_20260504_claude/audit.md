## Methodology notes

- **Level scale**: 0=design only, 1=scaffold, 2=integrated, 3=measured, 4=production-hardened.
- **PR106 compat**: `orthogonal` = stacks alongside; `additive` = adds new bytes for new gain; `replace` = swaps a current PR106 component; `obsolete` = superseded; `unknown` = needs further analysis.
- **Predicted Δ band**: bracket on score change vs PR106 0.20946 if integrated. Negative = score reduction = win.
- **EV score**: |midpoint of Δ band| ÷ hours. Higher = best ratio of expected gain to effort.
- **Conservative bias**: every band is conservative — actual results may exceed lower bound only if integration succeeds without unforeseen blockers.
- **GPU-cap constraint**: this audit was produced under $0 GPU cap, so all measurements reference prior `[contest-CUDA]` artifacts. Empirical re-validation is required before any claim on PR106 stack.
