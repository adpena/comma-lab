No radial gradients, no "ambient pulse" animations, no glassmorphism that screams AI-designed.

**Why:** The user wants the site to look like it was designed by a thoughtful human, not generated. Credibility with judges and hiring managers depends on it feeling intentional and understated, not flashy.

**How to apply:** Use flat dark backgrounds, generous whitespace, clean typography hierarchy, subtle borders. Think research paper meets Bloomberg terminal, not startup landing page. Symmetry and layout balance matter more than effects.
