Writeup and all docs must be free of corny, boastful, marketing, or AI-sounding language.
The tone should be smart but understated, friendly, and human.

**Why:** User's explicit preference. The design and writing should reflect genuine expertise
without trying to impress.

**How to apply:** Before finalizing any writeup text, scan for phrases like "nearly cost us
everything", "the single most important", "key trick", "core mechanism", "critical negative
finding", superlatives, and exclamation marks. Replace with plain, factual language. Let
the results speak for themselves.
