## The Core Thesis

A human with ZERO domain knowledge in video compression, neural codecs, steganalysis,
or computer vision — armed with curiosity, passion, will, and the ability to structure
and channel AI intelligence — produced results competitive with domain experts who
likely also use AI tools.

The difference is not the AI. The difference is the FRAMING.

## What We Actually Did: Meta-Perfect Steganalysis

We didn't just do inverse steganalysis against the scorers. We did meta-steganalysis
against EVERYTHING:

- **Yousfi's background**: His thesis advisor (Fridrich), his DDELab publications,
  his comma10k-baseline repo, his steganalysis expertise → revealed the scorer
  architecture and training methodology
- **Fridrich's life work**: 30 years of steganalysis → the "inverse steganalysis"

  submission format → every design choice reveals the organizer's intent
- **Quantizr's submission**: PR #53, FP4 codebook, marshal-obfuscated architecture,
  "mask2mask" name → reverse-engineered the winning paradigm
- **Cross-discipline integration**: Steganalysis + information theory + control theory +
  optimization + computer vision + video coding + robotics + neural rendering

## The Methodology: Structured Evocation

The human's role was NOT to have domain knowledge. It was to:
1. Ask the RIGHT questions ("what would Fridrich think?")
2. Structure the AI's reasoning ("convene the skunkworks council")
3. Demand rigor ("recursive review until 3 clean passes")
4. Channel curiosity ("research Yousfi's GitHub, DDELab, openpilot PRs")
5. Refuse to accept limitations ("fix ALL issues regardless of severity")
6. See patterns across domains ("the contest IS inverse steganalysis")

The AI's role was to be the greatest expression of intelligence and creativity
when properly framed, channeled, embodied, and structured by human intent.

## Why This Works Better Than Domain Experts + AI

Domain experts have PRIORS. Priors are efficient but they create blind spots:
- A video compression expert would optimize codecs, not train neural renderers
- A neural rendering expert would focus on architecture, not test-time optimization
- A steganalysis expert would analyze the scorer, not meta-analyze the organizer

Zero domain knowledge + structured AI exploration has NO blind spots. Every path
is equally plausible until evidence eliminates it. The skunkworks council — virtual
experts from 13 different domains — provides the expertise without the priors.
