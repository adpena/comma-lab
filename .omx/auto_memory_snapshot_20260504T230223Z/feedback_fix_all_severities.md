## The Rule

Fix ALL issues found in reviews, regardless of severity. Critical, important, AND suggestions — every single one.

**Why:** "regardless of severity we always implement all out of an abundance of caution" — the user views every issue as a potential signal loss or future bug. Skipping suggestions is not acceptable.

**How to apply:** When a greenup review or DX/UX audit produces findings, fix ALL of them before marking the pass as clean. Do not triage by severity. Do not defer suggestions. Fix everything in the same pass.
