# Research-Basis Contract - 2026-05-06 Codex

## Purpose

Added a machine-readable source map for paper-derived planning priors:

- `src/tac/optimization/research_basis.py`
- `src/tac/tests/test_research_basis.py`

The contract records source title, authors, year, venue/status, URL, lineage,
local paradigms, local variables, contest terms, charged-byte contract, and
hardening blockers.

## Safety

Every source is `planning_only`. Paper priors may shape atom ranking,
field-equation variables, entropy contexts, foveation geometry, pose priors, or
Bayesian design, but they cannot promote, rank, or claim contest score without:

- charged-byte archive custody;
- deterministic inflate/runtime proof;
- exact CUDA auth eval on the candidate archive;
- interaction review for stacks.

## Extensibility

`REQUIRED_SOURCE_FIELDS` makes this a schema, not a one-off list. Additional
project-researched papers can be added only if they declare source metadata,
lineage, local variables, contest terms, charged-byte contract, and blockers.
