## Non-Negotiable Claim Rules

- CUDA auth eval on exact archive bytes is the only score truth for GPU/scorer
  claims.
- MPS, CPU, Modal-CPU, local proxy, byte-only, smoke, stale-log, and memory-only
  outputs cannot promote, rank, kill, retire a family, validate a stack, or
  anchor paper claims.
- `contest_auth_eval.json` beats human logs and provenance parser fields.
- Negative exact evidence can retire a measured implementation/config only.
  Family/method kills require independent exact evidence or a mathematical
  impossibility argument plus clean Grand Council consensus.
- Dykstra/ADMM is a feasibility discipline. Additive deltas cannot be composed
  until standalone exact evals and a stacked exact eval exist.
